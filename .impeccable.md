# Design Context

## Target Audience
Office workers and administrators booking meeting rooms in a corporate environment. Mix of tech-savvy and non-technical users who need fast, frictionless scheduling.

## Use Cases
- Browse available rooms by floor, capacity, equipment
- Book a meeting room for a specific time range
- See room occupancy at a glance before committing
- Cancel or manage own bookings
- Admin: manage rooms, users, all bookings, system settings

## Brand Personality / Tone
- **Warm and approachable** — closer to Notion than Linear
- **Confident but not aggressive** — orange/amber warmth, not cold blue
- **Clean and functional** — productivity tool, not a showcase

## Design System
- **Font**: Plus Jakarta Sans (400, 500, 600, 700)
- **Color palette**: HSL with warm orange hue (25-40)
  - Primary: hsl(25, 80%, 52%) — warm orange
  - Background: hsl(40, 20%, 99%)
  - Foreground: hsl(30, 10%, 15%)
  - Muted: hsl(35, 15%, 95%)
  - Destructive: hsl(0, 65%, 48%)
  - Sidebar: hsl(25, 15%, 12%) — warm dark
- **Framework**: Nuxt 4 + shadcn-vue (reka-ui) + Tailwind CSS 3.4
- **Border radius**: 0.625rem
- **Aesthetic**: Notion-like list views, warm neutrals, no card-in-card nesting
